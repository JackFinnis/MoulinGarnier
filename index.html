The College is committed to fostering a working environment where individuals are treated with courtesy and respect. Student questionnaire responses must not be used to insult members of staff or other students, and comments which may cause offence will be removed from the results. It is also important to be mindful of unconscious bias and to avoid feedback shaped by stereotypes of gender, ethnicity or other protected characteristics.

While this questionnaire is confidential, you should avoid self-identification by referring to personal or other identifying information in your free text comments. To ensure that all feedback is taken on board, your responses will be considered for all modules, regardless of the number of students registered on each module.

Thank you for taking the time to participate in the MEQ. Your feedback will help to shape education here at Imperial for the better.
